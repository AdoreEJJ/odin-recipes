Passion Fruit Margarita

About this drink

Want to feel like you're on vacation? Try this drink with bold tropical flavors I know you'll love

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon coarse salt
  • 1 lime wedge
  • 1 1/2 cups ice
  • 5 ounces passion fruit juice
  • 2 fluid ounces silver tequila
  • 3/4 fluid ounces triple sec
  • 3/4 fluid ounces lime juice

Instructions

  1. sprinkle salt onto a plate, moisten the rim of glass with lime salt, rub glass in salt
  2. fill a cocktail shaker half-full wit ice. Add passion fruit juice, tequila, triple sec, and lime juice to the shaker. Seal and shake until outside is frosted.
