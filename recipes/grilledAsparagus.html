Grilled Asparagus

About This Dish

This is the best summer side dish. Cooks in minutes and becomes absolutely tender

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh asparagus
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. preheat outdoor grill on high and lightly oil grate
  2. ligthly coat the asparagus with olive oil, season with salt and pepper to taste
  3. cook asparagus on grill turning often until lightly charred(3-5 minutes)
