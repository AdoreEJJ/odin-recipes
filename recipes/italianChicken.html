Italian Chicken Marinade

About this dish

This is a very easy to follow recipe that is super tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle of italian-style salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Instructions

  1. whisk salad dressing, garlic powder, and salt together in a baking dish. Coat chicken
  2. preheat outdoor grill on high and oil grate
  3. remove chicken from marinade and discard the remaining marinade
  4. cook chicken about 7-8 minutes on each side or until the center is no longer pink and the juices are clear
